Shotokan Karate at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette is a club sport and a student organization, under the auspices of the All South Karate Federation and the Japan Karate Association. We offer beginning, intermediate, and advanced training to UL students, faculty, and staff. You are welcome to look at our club photos, events, and schedules, and links to other university shotokan clubs. For more information on joining the UL Karate Club, send and email to one of the club officers, or visit us at the scheduled training times.You are encouraged to be one of the founding members of our organization.

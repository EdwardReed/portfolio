About S.P.E.A.K.


The "Society for Peace, Environment,
Action, and Knowledge" is student
environmental action club at the
University of Louisiana - Lafayette.
We have an open General Interest
Weekly meeting on FRIDAYs at 1:15pm
in Room 116 of Hamilton Hall.
